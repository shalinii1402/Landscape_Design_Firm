Common Questions

We believe that informed clients make the best partners. Browse through our most frequently asked questions to learn more about our timelines, construction management, and how we handle everything from permitting to completion.

How long does a typical project take?

Most residential projects move from consultation to final installation in 8 to 14 weeks, depending on complexity and material lead times.

Do you handle the entire construction process?

Yes. We provide a "turn-key" service, managing everything from architectural design and permitting to full-scale construction and planting.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

The initial 20-minute discovery call is free. On-site analysis and conceptual layout proposals are part of our paid Design Commencement phase.
