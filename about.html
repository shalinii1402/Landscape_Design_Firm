A Legacy of Botanical Excellence

Founded in 2010 by a collective of landscape architects and environmental scientists, GreenScape was born from a simple yet profound realization: most modern landscapes were becoming detached from their natural surroundings.

We spent our first decade pioneering the concept of "Ecological Integration"—a design method that treats the garden as a seamless extension of the local environment. By studying micro-climates and indigenous soil health, we began creating luxury landscapes that didn't just look good, but thrived with minimal intervention.

Today, our firm is recognized globally for its minimalist aesthetic balanced with rich, wild biodiversity. We remain committed to our founding mission: building outdoor spaces that inspire, heal, and endure for generations.
